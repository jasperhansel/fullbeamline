The tk driver

The tk driver combines the xwin driver (see the section called “The xwin driver”) with Tcl/Tk to implement the tk device. So it shares the weaknesses (ugly graphics and text) of the xwin device as well as its strength (excellent raw interactive capability). Also unlike our other interactive devices the tk device provides GUI capabilities such as orient and zoom and a cmap0 and cmap1 palette editor.
