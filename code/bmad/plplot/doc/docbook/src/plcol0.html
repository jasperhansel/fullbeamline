plcol0: Set color, cmap0

plcol0 (icol0);
 

Sets the color index for cmap0 (see the section called “Color Map0”).

icol0 (PLINT, input)

Integer representing the color. The defaults at present are (these may change):

0 black (default background)
1 red (default foreground)
2 yellow
3 green
4 aquamarine
5 pink
6 wheat
7 grey
8 brown
9 blue
10 BlueViolet
11 cyan
12 turquoise
13 magenta
14 salmon
15 white

Use plscmap0 to change the entire cmap0 color palette and plscol0 to change an individual color in the cmap0 color palette.

Redacted form: plcol0(icol0)

This function is used in examples 1-9, 11-16, 18-27, and 29.
